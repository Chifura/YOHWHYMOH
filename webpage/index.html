Ramen Odyssey

"Savor the Flavors, Indulge in Delight"

MENU

Ramen

  • Tonkotsu Ramen: Rich pork broth with noodles, chashu pork, bamboo shoots, and a seasoned soft-boiled egg.
  • Shoyu Ramen: Soy sauce-based broth with noodles, sliced chicken, fish cake, nori, and green onions.
  • Miso Ramen: Miso-infused broth with noodles, ground pork, bean sprouts, corn, and a marinated soft-boiled egg.
  • Vegetarian Ramen: Vegetable broth with noodles, tofu, mushrooms, spinach, bamboo shoots, and seaweed.

Rice Meals

  • Teriyaki Chicken Donburi: Grilled chicken glazed in teriyaki sauce served over steamed rice with pickled vegetables.
  • Katsu Curry: Crispy breaded pork cutlet served with Japanese curry and rice.
  • Chirashi Sushi: Assorted sashimi over a bed of sushi rice, garnished with avocado, tobiko, and cucumber.
  • Oyakodon: Simmered chicken and egg served over rice with onions and dashi broth.

Snacks and Sides

  • Onigiri: Triangular rice balls filled with options like salmon, pickled plum (umeboshi), or grilled seaweed.
  • Takoyaki: Octopus-filled savory dough balls topped with takoyaki sauce, mayo, bonito flakes, and green onions.
  • Edamame: Steamed and salted young soybeans, a classic appetizer.
  • Gyoza: Pan-fried dumplings filled with pork, cabbage, and seasonings.

Japanese Desserts

  • Mochi Ice Cream: Soft and chewy rice cake filled with various ice cream flavors like green tea, mango, or red bean.
  • Matcha Parfait: Layers of matcha ice cream, red bean paste, mochi, and whipped cream.
  • Dorayaki: Sweet pancake-like buns filled with red bean paste.
